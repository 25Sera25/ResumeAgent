Job Title:  Senior Database Administrator

Onsite Requirement: A couple of days onsite required and travel to different sites. 

Job Summary: LCG is seeking a Senior Database Administrator (DBA) to lead the design, development, migration, and administration of modern SQL Server database systems in support of the client Applications Modernization Project. The Senior DBA will play a critical role in transitioning legacy DB2 systems to Microsoft SQL Server in a virtualized, scalable environment. This role also includes performance tuning, data modeling, backup strategy, and implementation of reporting services such as SSRS and SSAS.
